About Me

Hello! My name is Rachel, and I am a UX/UI designer and an Illustrator. In 2014, I moved from Santa Barbara to San Luis Obispo to attend California Polytechnic State Univeristy, San Luis Obispo. I will be graduating in June 2017 with a B.S. in Graphic Communication and a concentration in Web and Digital Media.

Aside for my love for good design, I love to hike, gym, watch netflix and sports, draw, eat and sleep. I love finding new places to explore and creating as many memories as possible.

My goals in life include working for a professional sports team or agency and inspiring others with my work.
