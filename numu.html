NUMU

User Experience Design

For my Digital Media class our project was to create a Music App with Ticket Purchase.The purpose of the app is to be able to play music from your library, support digital radio stations, include a social feed, and also allow concert tickets to be purchased.

I decided to call my app numu and base it off Spotify and Bandsintown. There's many apps out there similar to Spotify that allow you to play music, but what it doesn't include is a feature to find concerts near you or allow you to purchase concert tickets. I think an app that can include not only concert tickets, but also a library is beneficial for all music lovers. I also really love the social feed becuase it gives you access to others music and also raises awareness of other concerts near you.
