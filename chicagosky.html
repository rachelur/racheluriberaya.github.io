CHICAGO SKY WNBA TEAM

In April I began my creative services internship with the Chicago Sky. Since then I've learned many valuable tools that I will continue on with me. I used to be an illustrator only type of girl who only used Photoshop if I really had to. Now I love Photoshop and I've learned to utilize all the fun effects. I'm still learning, but that's the great part of using Illustrator, Photoshop or any of the other applications, there's always something new to learn. Since my internship began some of my favorite projects have been creative a give-away t-shirt, match up graphics and a few newsletter graphics along the way. I always knew I wanted to be in professional sports, and this internship has made me realize how much I enjoy it.

Some of my roles included:

  1. Using photoshop for game day graphics, flyers, and other social media graphics
  2. Taking photographs on game days, organizing them in their own files, and helping with the App
