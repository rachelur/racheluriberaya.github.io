TECHNICAL PAPER

Typography

For my GrC 218 Typography class we created a technical paper. In order to do this we used an article that interested us and I decided to choose sports. What made this project fun was using InDesign and learning how to use columns and how to make text continuous. I also loved the freedom we had in designing the layout. This project taught me most about threaded frames and how important white space is.

Some of my roles included:

  1. Chose an article and redesigned the layout.
  2. Recreated and designed layout.
